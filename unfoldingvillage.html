About Neri + Hu

Neri + Hu was established by Lyndon Neri and Rossana Hu in 2004. Neri+Hu is based out of Shanghai where they practice inter disciplinary architectural designs. They’re known for their Tsing Pu Retreat and Waterhouse at South Bund designs. They’re unique in the field as they’ve seeked minimally invasive ways to retain pre-existing structure as a way to retain parts of history. Thus, their designs often contain parts of pre-existing structure but refreshed with new features to give them purpose and functionality.
